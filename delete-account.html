Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your Eaves account and associated personal data at any time, whether or not you still have the app installed.

Option 1 — In the app (immediate)

If you have the app installed:

  1. Open Eaves and log in
  2. Go to Account → Privacy Settings → Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion

Your profile, photos, social connections, RSVPs, and role-specific data are removed immediately and permanently.

Option 2 — By email (no app required)

If you no longer have the app installed, or prefer to request deletion this way, email us:

Email eaves.help@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Your profile, uploaded photos, social connections (friends, follows), RSVPs, ratings, and role-specific data (artist, organizer, or venue owner profiles) are permanently deleted.

Exceptions: events or venues you created may remain visible, de-identified, so other users who engaged with them aren't affected. Where you were the subject of an administrative action (e.g. a report review), a de-identified log entry may be retained for up to 12 months for security purposes. Crash diagnostic data (Sentry) is retained for 30 days regardless.

Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
